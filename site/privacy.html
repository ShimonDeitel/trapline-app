Privacy Policy

Trapline is an offline-first app. All entries you log are stored locally on your device in the app's own storage area. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. The app does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party advertising SDKs.

The only network connection made by this app is to Apple's App Store servers to process in-app purchases (StoreKit), which is handled entirely by Apple.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is removed from your device.
